About Us

Digital Empowerment

Bono30 provides comprehensive programs and resources to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the digital age. By bridging the digital divide, we enable people to harness the power of technology and unlock their full potential.

Community Impact

Bono30 is committed to making a tangible difference in communities. We focus on driving positive change by improving lives, enhancing access to opportunities, and fostering sustainable development. Our initiatives are designed to uplift individuals and create lasting impacts that benefit the community as a whole.

Measurable Results

We believe in the power of measurable outcomes. Bono30 is dedicated to achieving tangible results through our efforts. By setting clear goals and tracking progress, we ensure that our initiatives have a meaningful and quantifiable impact on the lives of the individuals we serve.

Partnerships

  • Department of Social Development

    The Department of Social Development is a government agency that focuses on promoting social development and welfare programs. They aim to enhance the quality of life and well-being of individuals, families, and communities through various social support initiatives.

  • Micseta

    MICSETA (Media, Information and Communication Technologies Sector Education and Training Authority) is an organization responsible for the development and implementation of skills development programs in the media, information, and communication technology sectors. They collaborate with industry stakeholders to address skills gaps and ensure a competent workforce in these sectors.

  • Microsoft

    Microsoft is a multinational technology company known for developing and providing a wide range of software, hardware, and cloud-based services. They are a leading provider of operating systems, productivity software, enterprise solutions, and various other technologies. Microsoft plays a significant role in driving innovation and empowering individuals and organizations through their products and services.
